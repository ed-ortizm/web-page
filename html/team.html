Jeremy Tregloan-Reed is a Ph.D. in Astrophysics from the University of Keele (UK). His research interests include the detection and characterisation of exoplanets, spin-orbit alignment of planetary systems, starspot tracking in exoplanet light curves, stellar activity, exoplanetary atmospheres, and the impact of low Earth orbit communication satellite mega-constellations on astronomical observatories and the dark sky. He is a member of the INCT research line: Ciencias Planetarias y Exoplanetas.

Edgar Ortiz is a researcher at the Universidad de Antofagasta, Chile. He is an expert in satellite tracking and has published several papers on the subject.
